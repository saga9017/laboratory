Review: Judith Millers The Story: A Reporters Journey
In late 2002 and through 2003, Judith Miller, an investigative reporter at The New York Times, wrote a series of articles about the presumed presence of chemical and biological weapons and possible nuclear matriel in Iraq. Critics thought the articles too bellicose and in lock step with the George W. Bush administrations march to war. They all included careful qualifiers, but their overwhelming message was that Saddam Hussein posed a threat.
