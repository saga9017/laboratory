With Blistering Kick, Manchester City Rights a Choppy Season
The coach already knows he will not return. One top player seems to have a foot out the door. Much of the rest of the team are, understandably, unsure who will have a job and who will not come summer. And, with the Premier League title long since out of reach, there has been a palpable turn of the head toward the future for many of those in and around Manchester City during the past few months.
