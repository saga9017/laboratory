What the First Mixologist Honored by the James Beard Foundation Is Drinking Now
This May, the James Beard Foundation will induct Dale DeGroff, 66, into its Whos Who of Food & Beverage in America. Often credited as the grandfather of the modern cocktail revival, his trailblazing cocktail menu for the 1987 reopening of New Yorks Rainbow Room brought back to life the works of pre-Prohibition bartenders like Professor Jerry Thomas.
