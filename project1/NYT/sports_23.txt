For Most in Women's Soccer, Hashtags Don't Pay the Bills
To discuss the wage-discrimination debate unfolding at the top level of soccer in the United States, allow me to introduce Cheyenne, Abigail and Amaya. These three girls ? two 11-year-olds and a 10-year-old from the Jersey Shore ? put on their soccer jerseys, hopped into a minivan and traveled about an hour with their moms on Sunday afternoon to watch a women's professional soccer game, Sky Blue F.C.'s home opener here against the Washington Spirit.
